Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about OmniAI and its technology.

OmniAI integrates artificial intelligence directly into its blockchain protocol, enabling self-optimizing features that traditional cryptocurrencies can't match. Our AI continuously analyzes network conditions, security threats, and market dynamics to automatically adjust parameters for optimal performance.

Our AI-PoS consensus mechanism uses machine learning to select validators based on multiple factors beyond just stake amount, including historical performance, network contribution, and security metrics. This creates a more efficient and secure validation process compared to traditional PoS systems.

We're currently in discussions with several top-tier exchanges and expect listings to begin in Q4 2023 following our public sale. Exchange listings will be announced through our official channels once confirmed.

At launch, OMNI will be supported by MetaMask, Trust Wallet, and our official OmniAI Wallet. We're working on integrations with additional wallets and hardware wallets like Ledger and Trezor, which should be available by mainnet launch.

There are several ways to get involved: purchase OMNI tokens during our public sale, become a validator when mainnet launches, develop AI-powered dApps on our platform, or participate in our DAO governance. Join our community channels to stay updated on opportunities.
